Watermelon Lemonade

This Watermelon Lemonade is a light and refreshing summer drink that's easy to make with pureed watermelon and homemade lemonade. Garnish each glass with a slice of watermelon if you like.

Duration

The preparation time will be: 10m

The cook time will be: 5m

The Total time will be: 15m

Ingredients

Main Items

  • Watermelon
    • Substitute: Greenmelon
  • fresh lemon juice
    • Substitute: fresh apple juice
  • water
  • White sugar
  • cold water
  • ice cubes

Methods

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place watermelon into a blender;cover and puree until smooth. Strain through a fine mesh sieve.
  3. Bring sugar and 1/2 c water to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat until sugar dissolves, about 5 mts. Remove from heat;stir in 3 c of cold water and lemon juice.
  4. Divide ice into 12 glasses; scoop 2 to 3 tbsp of watermelon puree over ice, then top with lemonade.
  5. Gently stir before serving.

Cooking Terms Glossary

Cup
A volume measurement used primarily in the US and UK, equaling 8 fluid ounces (about 237 mL).
TBSP(Tablespoon)
A unit of volume equaling 3 tsp or 1/2 fluid ounce (about 15 mL).
Puree
Cooked food (usually fruits, vegetables, or legumes) that has been crushed, blended, or strained into a thick, smooth, and creamy paste.
Mesh sieve
An essential kitchen tool used for straining liquids, sifting dry ingredients, and washing produce.
